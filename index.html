About


Halló!

I am Einar, and I'm a photographer based in Europe. I love to travel around Iceland and Norway to capture beautiful landscapes and moments of nature.

My passion for photography started at the age of 15, when I bought my first DSLR camera after working at a electronic retailer. Just like most people, I began photography without having an understanding of the camera, and happily used the "automatic" settings. For years I took pictures just for fun but eventually I felt the urge to improve and understand how to properly use the camera. Now i spend most of my days exploring Europe and capturing the best moments I possibly can.
